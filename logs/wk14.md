## Reflect on Demo Day 

Demo day was really interesting.  It was great seeing the projects everyone created and the different directions people took their final projects.

Some projects were extremely interactive and engaging....they had smooth animations, responsive interfaces, 3D pages,  and really well and easy user experience. It was impressive to see how much interactivity some people built. But what I also liked was that people created a took that would actually be useful for everyday tasks. There were planners, organizers, and productivity apps that solved real problems that we face. Then there were people who went the creative route....I saw things like a penalty shootout game, which was entertaining and showed how you could use web development for fun. 

One of the best parts of demo day was actually talking to everyone and hearing about their process. Getting to understand how different people did their projects, what challenges they faced, what technical decisions they made, and what they learned along the way was great. Hearing their thought process made me appreciate the work even more.
